What's the name? L'OMBRE DU LYS

It's a? Eau De Parfum

It is impossible to extract lily's aroma in its natural state, but Master Perfumer Alberto Morillas found a way to construct an intoxicating fragrance that’s centered around the flower. An accord of green florals, neroli, and orange blossom softens the sultriness of tuberose while the white musk merges with soft notes of sandalwood. It’s a sensual olfactory delight that grabs hold of the senses and won’t let go.

A Bird On A Green Tea Leaf L'eau de Memo, which was created to celebrate the 10th anniversary of Memo Paris, encapsulates the spirit of the brand. On the perfume bottle, birds, symbol of travel and freedom, take flight from a sky that could be that of Paris, the cradle of Memo. The composition is unique and bears witness to an ongoing desire for creation. This fresh, intense and leathery eau de parfum is the result of a harmonious combination of green tea, jasmine absolute and leather.
